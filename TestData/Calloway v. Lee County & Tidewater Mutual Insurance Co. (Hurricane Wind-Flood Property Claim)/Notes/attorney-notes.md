## Theory of the case
Tidewater denied the claim on a **flood exclusion**. Our position: the predominant cause was **wind-driven rain through a failed roof**, not surface flood, so the exclusion shouldn't bar the dwelling claim (concurrent-causation / efficient-proximate-cause argument). The County angle is separate: the **Coronado Canal stormwater pump station #7** allegedly went offline during the storm and backed water into the neighborhood.
